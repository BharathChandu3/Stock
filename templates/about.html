About This App

This Stock Prediction App leverages cutting-edge machine learning to help users analyze and predict future stock prices. With a sleek UI and powerful backend algorithms, it simplifies financial forecasting for both beginners and experts.

Our Mission

We aim to democratize financial forecasting using the power of artificial intelligence. Our goal is to empower individuals with predictive insights and enable smarter investment decisions.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Backend: Python (Flask)
  • ML Model: Regression & Deep Learning on stock market data
  • Visualization: Chart.js, Particles.js

How It Works

This app collects historical stock data and uses ML models to identify patterns. It considers volume, volatility, and market sentiment to predict future prices with intuitive UI graphs and real-time outputs.
